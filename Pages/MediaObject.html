Sunrisers Hyderabad

The Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) are a franchise cricket team based in Hyderabad, Telangana, India, that plays in the Indian Premier League (IPL). The team is currently captained by David Warner and coached by Trevor Bayliss. The team made their first IPL appearance in 2013. The Sunrisers won their maiden IPL title in the 2016 season.
